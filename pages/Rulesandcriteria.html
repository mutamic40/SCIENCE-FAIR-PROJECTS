Computer Science Fair 2025

Rules

  • All projects must be original and created by the group members.
  • Each group must have a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 5 members.
  • Projects must be submitted before the deadline for eligibility.
  • All groups must be ready to present and explain their project during the fair.
  • Inappropriate or offensive content is strictly prohibited.
  • Use of external libraries or tools must be acknowledged.

Judging Criteria

  • Creativity and Innovation – Uniqueness of the idea and solution.
  • Technical Implementation – Functionality and use of technology.
  • Presentation – Clarity, structure, and engagement of the presentation.
  • Teamwork – Collaboration and group coordination.
  • Practical Application – Relevance and real-world use.
  • Documentation – Clear explanation of the project development process.
